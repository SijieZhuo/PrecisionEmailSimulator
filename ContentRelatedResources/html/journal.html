Introducing Materials Today Electronics

Learn more

 

We are delighted to introduce the newest member of the Materials Today family - Materials Today Electronics.

Edited by Professor Junhao Chu (Fudan University, China), this new open access journal publishes cutting-edge research work spanning fundamental and applied areas of electronic materials and devices, and system applications.

Topics include:

  • Energy electronics
  • Information electronics
  • Bioelectronics
  • Optoelectronics
  • Magnetic electronics
  • Acoustics electronics
  • Thermo electronics
  • Sensing electronics
  • Flexible electronics
  • Intelligent electronics
  • Applied research in electronics

The journal also considers papers on new function realization or enhancement of electronic devices.

Whats more, the article publishing charge is currently waived for all submissions before December 31, 2022.

What is Materials Today?
Materials Today is a collection of titles providing comprehensive coverage across materials science. This includes the flagship Materials Today journal (Journal Impact Factor 31.041*) as well as an extended family of publications. The family includes an expanding series of high quality specialist companion titles, publishing full length original research articles, short communications and reviews.Click here to find out more.
